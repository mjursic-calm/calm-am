Privacy Policy

All calmAM Apps — Last updated June 2026

All calmAM apps are designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data Collection

calmAM apps do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information entered into an app, including check-in entries, student records, and notes, is stored exclusively on your device using local browser storage. No data ever leaves your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties, because no user data is ever collected by us in the first place.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time using the "Clear all entries" function within the app, or by uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data permanently.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the apps evolve. Continued use of the apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact Michael Jursic at mjursic@gmail.com.
